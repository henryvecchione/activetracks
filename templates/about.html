FAQ

Why can't I get the songs during old activities?

The Spotify API limits the number of recently played songs it can return to 50, so ActiveTracks only works on activities within that 50-song window. Nothing I can do about this one for the time being.

Why don't some songs show up?

Again, direct your attention to the Spotify API, which only counts songs as "recently played" if they are played to completion. And again, nothing I can do.
